# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

This project adopts Contributor Covenant version 2.1.

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age,
body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and
expression, experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, appearance, race, caste,
color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We will act in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy
community.

## Expected behavior

Examples include empathy and kindness, respect for differing viewpoints, constructive feedback,
accepting responsibility, and focusing on what is best for the community. Unacceptable behavior
includes sexualized language or attention, trolling or insults, public or private harassment,
publishing private information without permission, and conduct inappropriate in a professional
setting.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers clarify and enforce standards and may remove, edit, or reject contributions or
participation that violate them. Report conduct concerns privately to `conduct@mindpool.io`.
Maintainers will respect reporter privacy and apply consequences proportionate to impact, from a
correction or warning through temporary or permanent exclusion. Retaliation is prohibited.
